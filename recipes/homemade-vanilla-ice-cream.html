Homemade Ice Cream

Description

A recipe on how to make Ice Cream at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of Heavy Cream
  • 1 1/2 cups of Whole Milk
  • 2/3 cups of Cane Sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of Vanilla Extract
  • 1/8 teaspoons of Sea Salt

Steps

  1. Firtsly, in a medium saucepan, combine your heavy cream, whole milk, cane sugar, vanilla extract, and sea salt together. Warm over medium-low heat, whisking often, for 5 minutes, or until the sugar dissolves and the mixture is warmed through.
  2. Once everything is stirred, pour into a heatproof bowl, cover, and chill for 2 hours or overnight.
  3. Remove the chilled ice cream base from the fridge and whisk well. Churn in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturerâ€™s instructions, typically 20 to 30 minutes. The ice cream will be soft after churning. For firmer ice cream, transfer to an airtight container and freeze for 2 to 4 hours before serving.
  4. Don't forget; Toppings are optional if you want to enjoy your homemade ice cream with something as simple as feshly cut strawberries.
