Funfetti Mug Cake

Description

This is a short 5 minute recipe for a funfetti mug cake.

Ingredients

  • One large egg
  • 2 tablespoons of Vegetable Oil
  • 3 tablespoons of Whole Milk
  • 1/2 teaspoons of Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 cup of All-purpose Flour
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of Sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of Sprinkles

Steps

  1. To start, in a large, microwave-safe mug, whisk together one egg, vegetable oil, milk, and vanilla.
  2. Then, whisk in your flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Afterwards, stir in your sprinkles.
  4. Be sure to microwave your mug cake on full power for 1 minute, or until your cake tests done.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
